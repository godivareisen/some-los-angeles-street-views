Between 1962 and 1978, Ed Ruscha published sixteen artists books, featuring his deadpan photographs that depict exactly what the books’ titles describe. Some Los Angeles Street Views revisits Ruscha's 1965 book entitled Some Los Angeles Apartments, juxtaposing the street views that the artist composed, with the most recent Google Street Views of the same subjects. A lot (or very little) can happen in 50 years…
